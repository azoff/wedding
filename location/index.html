Suncadia, Cle Elum, Washington

Suncadia is a beautiful, adventure-filled wilderness resort 1.5 hours east of Seattle.

May 27 is the Sunday of Memorial Day weekend. Be sure to make your travel accommodations (flight, car, lodging) ahead of time.

    Getting There

    arrive safe and sound

    May 27, 2018 is the Sunday of Memorial Day weekend. We recommend you plan and make any necessary reservations sooner-rather-than-later.

    GETTING TO SUNCADIA

    Nearest Airport: SeaTac Airport Suncadia is approximately 1.5 hours east of Seattle (on Washington Interstate I-90, East Bound)

    • Rental cars are available at SeaTac airport
    • Airporter Shuttles are available from SeaTac to Suncadia

    LEAVING SUNCADIA

    We will never want the long-weekend to end! When the time comes, remember that you may run into traffic. Please plan driving time accordingly.

    Getting Around

    explore the area

    If you are staying at Suncadia Resort, there are walking/running/biking trails all around the resort (plus golf, swimming, spa, wine tasting, and more). Click here for more details.

    There is also a free intra-resort shuttle service that can bring you to/from all events and activities.

    If you are staying outside of Suncadia, there are a couple local taxis available. Uber and Lyft are limited. - K.C. Cab: (509) 929-3055
